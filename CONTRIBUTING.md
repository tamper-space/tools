# Contributing

This repository is dual-licensed: GPLv3 for open use, with commercial licenses
available for proprietary embedding (see [COMMERCIAL.md](COMMERCIAL.md)). Keeping
that possible requires two things from every contribution, and both are hard gates
on merging.

## 1. Sign-off (DCO)

Every commit must carry a `Signed-off-by` line (`git commit -s`) certifying the
[Developer Certificate of Origin 1.1](https://developercertificate.org/): you wrote
the change or otherwise have the right to submit it under this project's license.

## 2. License grant

By submitting a contribution you license it under GPL-3.0-only and additionally
grant the maintainer (the tamper.space project) a perpetual, irrevocable,
worldwide, royalty-free right to reproduce, modify, and distribute your
contribution under other license terms, including commercial licenses. You keep
the copyright to your contribution; this grant is what lets the project remain
dual-licensed with your change in it.

If you cannot or do not want to make this grant, do not submit the change.
Pull requests are treated as agreement to both sections.
